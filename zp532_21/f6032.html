AMEERAT AL ARAB - ASDAAF

種類...オードパルファム

総容量...100ml

容器の形状...スプレー

Asdaaf Ameerat Al Arab cologne opens with a bursts of citrus. The bergamont and other citruses create a zesty and refreshing combination. The heart notes evolve with white musk and aloe vera, adding an earthy and sweet animalistic scent to the profile. Finally, the base mixes white florals with oud to create a powdery and rich fragrance.
